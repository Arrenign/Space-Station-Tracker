ISS Tracker

ISS Tracker is an android application that allows you to easily track the position of the International Space Station (ISS) and find out what astronauts are currently in space. The application comes with a notification system that can alert you whenever ISS approaches your location and/or the astronauts in space change. The system will also try to give you a heads up before ISS approaches your location by bringing live data right into your notification bar.
